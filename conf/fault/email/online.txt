A subsystem has gone offline. This most often applies to antennas, though
it can correspond to other subsystems (such as correlator bands) as well.
You may need to restart the computer controlling this subsystem.

Look here for fixes for Sza, Ovro and Bima telescopes respectively:
http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Alarms#SzaX.online
http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Alarms#OvroX.online
http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Alarms#BimaX.online
