This alarm is triggered when the system has not made an integration in the
last 14 minutes.  The most likely reason for this is that you need to
schedule a project. However, if this error was triggered during a project,
please see the wiki for additional information:

http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Alarms#Wbpipeline.IntegratorStageContainer.IntegratorStage.timeSinceLastIntegration
http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Alarms#Slpipeline.IntegratorStageContainer.IntegratorStage.timeSinceLastIntegration

Look here for notes on how to schedule projects:

http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Scheduling_Projects
