The third stage temperature for the cryogenic system is high. 
This may be indicative of a warm up of the 4k stage and should be carefully
watched. There can be multiple causes, including contamination and worn seals,
refrigerator motor failure, compressor failure, or low helium supply pressure. 

See also:
http://cedarflat.mmarray.org/wiki/Alarms#BimaX.BimaSpecific.Dewar.stage3
